Root cause thing


Students drop out because programming is hard to learn

High dropout rate but using fun lego robots decreased dropout rate and increased flowchart and structure literacy in homework


When students aren’t having fun, they won't work as hard to learn a subject. Having the fun lego robots made students more willing to learn, decreased dropouts, and helped the students’ skills. The students were able to connect to computer science through the use of robots, allowing them to achieve greater things.


Fewer people want to major in cs over time, groups of people are noticing this and trying to get more people interested in cs


Students don’t want to do cs, so people are doing studies on making electives to increase interest in computers.


This implies that it is important that students connect to cs classes. Students need to be able to take classes that will teach them but also connect to previous and other experiences, so they can stay interested and willing to learn in cs.
